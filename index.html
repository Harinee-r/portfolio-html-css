A Little About Me

I am a third year student of B.Design, specializing in UI/UX and Interaction Design at PES University, based in Bangalore. I’ve honed my ability to create intuitive, research-driven designs that are both user-friendly and visually appealing. I’m passionate about minimal and modern design, always backed by deep research to create experiences that connect with users on a personal level. I like to think that the best designs are the ones you barely notice. Besides designing, I also enjoy sketching, crochet and baking. I’m driven by a love for learning and collaboration, always eager to take on challenges and find the best solutions for any project I’m part of.
